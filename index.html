About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fend off endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, collect experience to level up, and unlock powerful new skills to stay alive as long as possible in this thrilling arcade experience.

Inspired by popular roguelike survival games, Monster Survivors offers a simple yet addictive gameplay loop that keeps you coming back for more. How long can you survive against the monster hordes?

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters in an endless survival challenge that tests your skills and strategy.

Character Progression

Level up your character by collecting experience, unlocking new abilities and improving your stats.

Various Enemies

Battle against a diverse array of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.

Upgradable Weapons

Find and upgrade different weapons and items to create powerful combinations for monster slaying.

Playable Characters

Choose from various characters, each with unique starting abilities and playstyles:

The Warrior

A balanced fighter with strong melee attacks and decent health. Perfect for beginners.

The Mage

Specializes in powerful area-of-effect spells but has lower health. Requires careful positioning.

The Ranger

Excels at long-range combat with rapid attacks. Maintains distance from enemies for survival.

How to Play

  1. Movement: Use WASD keys or arrow keys to move your character
  2. Attacks: Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range
  3. Leveling: Collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters
  4. Upgrades: Choose new abilities or upgrades when you level up
  5. Survival: Stay alive as long as possible and aim for a high score!
